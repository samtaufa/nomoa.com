Computer Studies Course Notes

Course notes material for beginning students in Junior/Senior High School students.

This section of Tonga on the 'NET contains course-notes and material developed for Junior/Senior High computer studies courses for Form 4, Form 5, and Form 6 in Tonga. As a test/development site for sharing information between staff and students these pages will continue to evolve to incorporate increase the content for other subjects and from student works.

Why Computers?

According to a study released by the Information Technology Association of America and Virginia Polytechnic Institute, approximately 10% of all IT positions-or 346,000 jobs-are going begging. To help close the gap, the Labor and Commerce departments plan to spend some $28 million to train and recruit newcomers to IT.

Supported Browsers

This section was originally designed for DHTML and Internet Explorer 4.0. I have spent some hours reworking it to work with Netscape 3.0 and 4.0 although some features seem to still work best with IE 4.0x.

Adobe Acrobat 3.0 Format

Some of these pages are created in Adobe Acrobat 3.0 Format. The PDF Acrobat format makes the information very easy to transport, print and to view off-line. To download the FREE Acrobat reader if you don't already have it, click on the yellow button on the left or goto Adobe's web site.
