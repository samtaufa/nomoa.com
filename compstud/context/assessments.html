Assessment Modules

Assessment modules collect together exercises designed to help evaluate your progress in a topic area. The objective of the exercises are to provide students and staff materials to use for skills development, and reference for further exercise development.

Exercises

Exercises are divided into their application groups. Select an application group you wish to see exercises for, and select the exercise you wish to see.

Some exercises are provided with marking schedules to assist students review their work, and to provide teachers with samples of marking alternatives.

Tests

Smalls 'paper' tests are included as examples, exercises for revision/assessment of knowledge achievement.

Adobe Acrobat Reader 3.01 is required for viewing a number of the files (PDF) on this site. If after you have selected an exercise the file does not show in your browser (or open up) then you need to install Acrobat Reader which can be obtained from Adobe. Netscape Navigator may require user configuration to make sure the Adobe Acrobat files open correctly.
