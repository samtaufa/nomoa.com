Computer Studies Course Notes

The materials in this section of the web pages are divided into SIX major categories of information.

  Category Further Information.
  Systems Takes a look at the different Computer Systems and "Systems" level information.
  Databases Provides definitions and exercises for Databases
  Programming Introduces computer programming using QBasic.
  Spreadsheets Provides definitions and exercises for Spreadsheets.
  Word-Processing Provides definitions and exercises for Word Processing.
  Other For those things that didn't quite fit in earlier
  Local Provides Computer Studies notes that are relevant locally for Tonga, may also be useful to others.

Select a Category to find more detailed topics, and select the topic to recieve the materials written on the topic.
