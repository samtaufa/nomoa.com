TimeTabling

There are a plethora of timetabling packages available on the market, and no-one is interested in buying one. By using some of the simpler algorithms (eg. backtracking) we can probably develop a small timetabling package that can be readily used at schools in Tonga.

The TimeTabling project can then expand to include some of the other features in full packages, such as scheduling examinations, staff load analysis/allocation.
